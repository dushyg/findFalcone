After the recent Falicornian war, King Shan has exiled Queen Al Falcone for 15 years. However, if he finds her before the 15 years are up, she has to go into exile for another 15 years! King Shan has received intelligence that Al Falcone is hiding in one of six neighbouring planets. To solve this problem this application helps you to choose the planets to search, and the vehicles to use in Finding Falcone. Please click on 'docs' link in the header for more information.
